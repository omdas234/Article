Difference of Frontend and Backend Development

I will help you understand this with an example. Friends, when you go to a shop to buy some product, you keep eating at the front of the shop. There you see the shop front and the shopkeeper. You ask him for things that you want. But does it happen that the shopkeeper immediately catches the things you asked for and gives them to you?

No! All the things are stored in the back of the shop. The shopkeeper finds the product you have asked for from the store and then gives it to you. This entire process of going to the shop to ask for something and the shopkeeper bringing it to you, takes place in two parts of the shop: front and back. At the front you are looking for the items you want to eat, and at the back, you are looking for the items stored in the store. The same way this shop works, the same way the website also works.

Shop Example

Whenever you visit any website, whatever you see, all that information is in front of site . There is also a backend of the website, which works like a shop. This was the difference between frontend and backend which we understood from the example of a shop.

Frontend Development

Let us understand how frontend development works and how the frontend of a website is created.

Friend, the entire website is made from code. The frontend of most of our sites is created using programming languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We will understand these 3 languages and will also see how they are used in the website:

  • HTML (HyperText Markup Language): This is the body or complete structure of the website. The house you see in the example is just a structure. Without it, you will not be able to find a house like this. By the way, without HTML, the body of the website will not be created.
  • CSS (Cascading Style Sheets): By using CSS, we makeover the body created from HTML. That means we have done its complete design. Like earlier, the house was just a body, but after applying CSS, it got a complete design: color, door, and other things which make the house beautiful.
  • JavaScript: With its help, we can control the website. Now you see, a house will not be just a design. The door that we have should also work, it should have light, and all the things that make the house livable should work. JavaScript helps in doing all this work.
